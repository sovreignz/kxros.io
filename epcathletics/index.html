Defining Qualifying Revenue (Attribution)

Performance compensation should reward measurable growth—not create ambiguity. Recommended structure:

Establish average monthly revenue before engagement. 25% applied to revenue generated above that baseline.

Strength: Accounts for overall business growth.
Consideration: Requires clear baseline and seasonality treatment.

25% applied to revenue generated directly through newly created assets (new funnels, specific links, ManyChat flows).

Strength: Creates direct attribution.
Consideration: Multi-channel touchpoints can complicate tracking.

Use both baseline and direct attribution to create the fairest assessment of growth.

  • Establish current revenue baseline
  • Track new revenue through dedicated links
  • Exclude clearly pre-existing recurring revenue
  • Define treatment for refunds, payment plans, and processing fees
Qualifying new revenue generally means collected revenue attributable to new/improved systems developed during the engagement, subject to final signed agreement.
